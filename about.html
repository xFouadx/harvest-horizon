About Us

Harvest Horizon is an Egyptian company founded in 2025, dedicated to connecting the rich agricultural heritage of Egypt with markets around the world. We specialize in sourcing fresh fruits and vegetables from small-scale farmers across the country, ensuring fair trade practices and promoting sustainable agriculture.

Our core focus is on exporting—managing the process with precision and care to guarantee that every shipment arrives fresh, compliant, and on-spec at the recipient’s port. From field to freight, we prioritize quality control, proper handling, and timely logistics.

At Harvest Horizon, we bridge the gap between Egypt’s fertile land and global demand, delivering produce that reflects our commitment to excellence, transparency, and reliability.
