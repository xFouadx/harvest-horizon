Medicinal and Aromatic Plants

Egyptian medicinal and aromatic plants (MAPs) stand out globally due to the country’s unique climate, fertile soils, and centuries-old agricultural heritage, which together produce exceptionally potent, naturally sun-dried herbs with rich essential oil content and superior purity — making them highly valued in international markets for their quality, aroma, and therapeutic strength.

Herbal Plants

Herbal plants offer natural healing and wellness benefits, valued for their therapeutic properties, rich aromas, and time-tested use in traditional and modern remedies.

Sweet Basil (Ocimum basilicum)

Dried basil delivers a concentrated aroma and flavor, capturing the herb’s sweet, slightly peppery essence—perfect for enhancing sauces, dressings, and Mediterranean dishes year-round.

Rosemary (Rosmarinus Officinalis)

Rosemary is a fragrant, woody herb prized for its pine-like aroma and potent antioxidant properties, making it a staple in both culinary and therapeutic applications.

Marjoram (Origanum Majorana)

Marjoram is a delicate, sweet herb with subtle floral and citrus notes, valued for its soothing aroma and gentle flavor in both culinary and herbal traditions.

Geranium (Pelargonium Graveolens L.)

Geranium is a highly aromatic plant known for its rosy, citrus-like scent and is prized for its essential oil, widely used in perfumery, skincare, and natural wellness remedies.

Floral Plants

Floral plants are celebrated for their vibrant colors and therapeutic properties, offering natural antioxidants, soothing compounds, and visual appeal in both herbal remedies and wellness products.

Roselle (Hibiscus Sabdariffa)

Roselle is a vibrant, tangy-flavored flower rich in antioxidants and vitamin C, widely used in herbal teas and wellness products for its refreshing taste and health-supporting benefits.

Marigold (Calendula Officinalis L.)

Marigold is a bright, golden-hued flower known for its gentle anti-inflammatory and skin-soothing properties, making it a valued ingredient in natural healing and cosmetic formulations.

Aromatic Seeds

Aromatic seeds are prized for their concentrated essential oils and distinctive scents, playing a key role in culinary, medicinal, and wellness applications through their rich flavors and therapeutic properties.

Fennel (Foeniculum Vulgare Mill)

Fennel is a sweet, licorice-scented plant prized for its aromatic seeds and digestive benefits, commonly used in herbal teas, culinary blends, and traditional remedies.

Coriander (Coriandrum Sativum)

Coriander is a versatile herb known for its citrusy aroma and flavorful seeds, widely used in cooking and traditional medicine for its digestive and antioxidant properties.

Caraway (Carum Carvi)

Cumin (Cuminum Cyminum)

Cumin is a bold, earthy spice with a warm, nutty flavor, renowned for its digestive properties and essential role in global culinary and traditional medicinal practices.

Anise (Pimpinella Anisum)

Anise is a sweet, licorice-flavored seed cherished for its soothing digestive effects and widely used in herbal infusions, confections, and traditional remedies.

Black Cumin (Nigella Sativa)

Black Cumin is a powerful seed known for its peppery flavor and centuries-old use in traditional medicine, valued for its immune-supporting, anti-inflammatory, and antioxidant properties.
